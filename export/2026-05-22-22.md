## MICONIC: The multiphase circumnuclear region of Centaurus A as seen with JWST/MIRI MRS observations. I. Spectral inventory and properties of the warm molecular disk
- ArXiv: 2605.22497v1
- Link: http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.22497v1
- Updated (UTC): 2026-05-21T13:48:13+00:00
- Authors: L. Evangelista, P. Guillard, J. Martin, P. Salomé, A. Alonso Herrero, L. Pantoni, L. Hermosa Muñoz, V. Buiten, A. Labiano, M. García-Marín, L. Colina, T. Böker, D. Dicken, M. J. Ward, G. Wright, P. van der Werf, S. Garcia-Burillo, M. Baes, A. Eckart, G. Östlin, D. Rouan, F. Walter, R. A. Riffel, M. Güdel
- Abstract: Supermassive black holes power Active Galactic Nuclei (AGN), injecting energy that regulates accretion and shapes host galaxies. We investigate the morphology, excitation, and kinematics of molecular hydrogen (H2) in the inner circumnuclear disk of Centaurus A, the nearest radio galaxy. We present JWST/MIRI MRS integral-field spectroscopy of the central 170x100 pc2 at 0.3"-0.7" (5-12 pc) resolution, focusing on pure rotational H2 lines. The spectra show strong nuclear continuum and bright H2 emission from S(1) to S(8), including the first S(8) detection in Centaurus A. Optically thin nuclear lines enable maps of temperature, column density, and ortho-to-para ratio from spaxel-level excitation-diagram fitting. Warm H2 shows a complex morphology, dominating the central region where CO emission is weak or undetected. Low-excitation H2 lines trace an inhomogeneous ring with a 20-pc-radius cavity aligned with the jet's near side, suggesting that the jet affects the morphology of the molecular disk. Higher-excitation lines form filamentary structures around the AGN. Kinematics are rotational with an S-shaped distortion, indicating non-circular motions or a warped disk. A coherent, low-dispersion (70 km/s) streamer spirals inward. A power-law temperature distribution yields a warm (100-2000 K) H2 mass of (5.6+/-1.4)e5 Msun and a dynamical mass of 5e8 Msun within 100 pc. Shock excitation is supported by enhanced H2/continuum and H2/PAH ratios, elevated [Ne III]/[Ne II], and sub-equilibrium ortho-to-para ratios (1.6-2.4). Turbulent dissipation can balance H2 cooling and likely dominates heating beyond 30 pc. In the inner 100 pc of Centaurus A, AGN feeding and feedback are linked: shocks excite H2, regulate the gas temperature, and prevent cooling below 100 K, explaining the weak CO emission and lack of a massive outflow. These shocks may drive angular momentum loss and help fuel the nucleus.

- 光谱分析：提取 H2 S(1)–S(8) 线，执行核线的光学薄处理，以实现逐像元的激发-diagram 拟合，获得温度、柱密度与 OPR 的图像。
- 形态学与动力学：基于线强度与比率构建不同激发态的空间分布图，识别低激发环、腔洞、丝状结构及螺旋向内流；通过线宽与位移场分析获得旋转性和非圆运动/扭曲的证据。

- 任务：系统地描绘 Cen A 内部圆盘区的分子氢形态、激发机制与动力学结构，以揭示在近端 AGN 环境中供给与反馈的耦合关系。
- 表现：通过高分辨率 JWST 数据实现对 H2 的全面测绘，首次检测到 H2 S(8)，并在逐像元水平获得温度、柱密度与 OPR 的空间分布；揭示环洞与射流相关的形态学特征、丝状与螺旋向内流的动力学；提供暖 H2 的质量与在 100 pc 内的 dynamical mass，以及证据支持的冲击激发和湍耗散在核心区域的主导作用。总体结论是，在 Cen A 的内 100 pc，AGN 的供给和反馈紧密相连，冲击驱动的加热抑制了过冷却，解释了弱 CO 发射和缺乏大规模外流的观测，并提出冲击可能推动角动量损失、促进核区燃料供给。

## There and back again? Neutral outflows in z~3.5 quiescent galaxies
- ArXiv: 2602.17767v2
- Link: http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.17767v2
- Updated (UTC): 2026-05-21T13:24:34+00:00
- Authors: Pengpei Zhu, Kei Ito, Francesco Valentino, Massissilia Hamadouche, Gianluca Scarpe, Katherine E. Whitaker, Takumi Kakimoto, William M. Baker, Anna R. Gallazzi, Steven Gillman, Rashmi Gottumukkala, Christian Kragh Jespersen, Minju Lee, Allison W. S. Man, Georgios Magdis, Masato Onodera, Rhythm Shimakawa, Aswin Vijayan, Po-Feng Wu
- Abstract: Neutral gas outflows play a crucial role in the baryon cycle of galaxies, their properties provide key insights into the transition from star formation to quiescence. In this work, we investigate the neutral gas outflow of 23 massive ($M_\star = 10^{10.1-11.6}\,\rm M_\odot$) quiescent galaxies (QGs) at z=2.82--4.61, selected from the JWST NIRSpec (R~1000) and NIRCam program DeepDive. We trace the neutral gas outflows using the NaI Doublet absorption lines and detect excess NaI D in 13/23 (57%) targets, of which 7/23 (30%) show blueshifted absorption with velocity offsets $|Δv|$ >~ 150 km/s. The z ~ 3.5 targets have $Δv$ similar to those of their local counterparts; they are also equivalent when compared in SFR--$Δv$ space. We derive mass outflow rates and identify the most extreme neutral gas outflow rate $\log(\dot M_{\rm out} / \mathrm{M_\odot \, yr}^{-1})=2.68\pm0.27$ beyond the local Universe, coincident with an X-ray AGN. For all NaI D detected systems, the inferred mass outflow rate can, in principle, suppress ongoing star formation; however, the outflows are unlikely to escape their hosts, suggesting recycling on relatively short timescales (~3--180 Myr), depending on the assumed potential and launching radius. All NaI D detected targets occupy the LI(N)ER region of the BPT diagram and/or are X-ray detected, but we find no strong correlation between ongoing AGN and the neutral outflow: 2/4 broad-line/X-ray AGNs are NaI D undetected -- yet, the outflows can be powered by fossil/episodic AGNs, and one broad-line target shows a possible P-Cygni profile that indicates strong outflows. As neutral outflows alone are not able to permanently quench star formation by removing gas in our sample at z ~ 3.5, the presence of gas cycling in and out of massive passive systems may instead be the signature of feedback-regulated quenching-maintenance processes.

**Summary**  
1. Research background  
- 中性气体外流在星系的重子循环与星形成向休眠的转变之间起着关键作用。  
- 近年来，利用高-redshift（z>2）观测来直接追踪寂静星系中的中性外流成为可能，尤其借助 JWST 提供的高灵敏度与分辨率。  
- 本研究聚焦在 z ~ 3.5 的大质量 QGs，通过 NaI D 吸收线来探测中性气体外流，并尝试揭示其对星形成抑制的作用及气体循环特征。

2. Prior methods and limitations  
- 先前的研究多集中于局部宇宙或星形成主序星系，针对高-z QGs 的中性外流探测较为困难，原因包括背景光谱较弱、信噪比受限以及对外流动力学与气体保留/循环的区分难度。  
- 对于高-z QGs，直接将外流与 AGN 活性、发射半径、势场等耦合以推导质量外流速率及逃逸可能性仍存在显著不确定性。  
- 与 AGN 的关系（持续性 vs. 化石/ episodic 的驱动）以及外流是否真正导致长期抑制星形成，也仍存在争议。

3. Main contributions  
- 在 z ~ 3.5 的 23 个高质量 QGs 中，通过 NaI D 吸收线检测到中性气体外流信号，13/23 靶标存在额外的 NaI D 吸收，7/23 显示蓝移吸收。  
- 记录到的极端外流速率达到 log(\dot M_out / M_sun yr^{-1}) = 2.68 ± 0.27，并且其中一个与 X-ray AGN 相符合。  
- 发现所有 NaI D 检测靶标位于 LI(N)ER 区域和/或被 X 射线探测，且总体上与持续性 AGN 的直接相关性不强；外流可能由化石/ episodic AGN 驱动，存在强外流的证据（如 P-Cygni 线型可能）。  
- 指出在 z ~ 3.5 时，中性外流若仅凭外流本身很难永久性抑制星形成，因为气体存在来回循环，且循环时间尺度依赖于势场与发射半径，约在 3–180 Myr 的范围内。  
- 结论为：中性外流并非单独实现“永久性”星形成抑制，而是与气体循环和反馈维持机制共同作用，提示在高-z 的大质量被动系统中，反馈调控的抑制-维持过程可能通过气体来回流动来实现。

4. Method overview  
- 数据与样本：来自 JWST 的 NIRSpec（R~1000）与 NIRCam DeepDive 项目，选取 redshift 2.82–4.61 的 23 颗高质量 QGs。  
- 观测与分析：通过 NaI D 双线吸收来追踪中性气体外流，测定吸收强度、蓝移分量及速度偏移 Δv，判定外流的存在与动力学。  
- 物理量推导：在假设的势场和发射半径条件下，推导质量外流速率 \dot M_out，并讨论其在星形成抑制中的潜在作用与是否能逃逸宿主。  
- 结果解读：将外流与 AGN 活性、BPT LI(N)ER 区位、以及 X 射线探测等信息结合，评估外流驱动源及持续性抑制的证据。并讨论外流的可回流性质及对星形成的长期影响。

5. Task and performance  
- 任务：评估 z ~ 3.5 时期大质量 QGs 的中性气体外流特征，以及外流对星形成抑制的有效性与气体循环的存在性。  
- 关键发现与性能指标：  
  - NaI D 检测率为 13/23（57%），其中 7/23（30%）展现蓝移吸收，Δv 需 ≳ 150 km/s。  
  - 极端外流速率达到 log(\dot M_out / M_sun yr^{-1}) = 2.68 ± 0.27。  
  - 外流对星形成的抑制在理论上成立，但不大可能完全将气体从宿主星系中移除，短-中时标的循环回流（约 3–180 Myr）更符合观测。  
  - 所有 NaI D 检测靶标均落在 LI(N)ER 区域与/或被 X 射线探测，但 AGN 的持续性活跃性与中性外流之间未发现强相关性；外流可能由化石/ episodic AGN 提供能量，一例靶标可能存在 P-Cygni 外流证据。  
- 结论：中性外流本身不足以在 z ~ 3.5 的样本中实现永久性星形成抑制；相反，气体的来回循环在被动系统中可能是反馈调控的抑制-维持机制的标志。

## Single-source-class interpretation of the diffuse astrophysical neutrino flux
- ArXiv: 2603.15754v2
- Link: http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.15754v2
- Updated (UTC): 2026-05-21T09:00:01+00:00
- Authors: Walter Winter, Damiano F. G. Fiorillo, Sara Buson
- Abstract: We explore the interpretation that the diffuse astrophysical neutrino flux is dominated by a single standard candle-like source class. Since recent observations favor a broken power law with a spectral break around 30 TeV, we postulate that the $pγ$ channel is the dominant neutrino production process creating a peak at these energies. We use a SOPHIA-based photo-pion interaction model with a thermal target including high-energy processes, such as multi-pion production, which turns out to be relevant for the interpretation. We demonstrate that target photon temperatures 0.1 to 1 keV are preferred in a multi-parameter fit, whereas the maximal neutrino energies can be limited by A) soft injection spectra, B) a maximal proton energy in the PeV range, or C) magnetic field effects on the secondary muons, pions, and kaons with B in the few 10 kG range. We predict that future measurements, such as of the neutrino flavor composition or neutrino-antineutrino ratio (Glashow resonance), can discriminate scenarios. We also point out that the parameters obtained in our generic approach, such as in the strong magnetic field values, might be indicative for an AGN core origin as a driver of the diffuse flux.

4. 方法概览

## Investigating central star formation in local AGN host galaxies: is there tension between coeval growth and AGN feedback?

- Abstract: It has been argued that supermassive black holes (BHs) coevolve with the central parts of galaxies, as a result of the common fuel for both the BH and star formation in the galaxy central region, as supported by the particularly significant relation between BH growth and the central mass density within 1 kpc found among star-forming galaxies. - 对于峰位与曲率之间的关系、以及不同类别的冷却机制差异，仍存在争议和不确定性。

3. 主要贡献  
- 为 93 个 Fermi blazars 构建了准同时的宽带 SEDs，并对两峰进行了允许非对称性的三次拟合。  
- 系统揭示了 ν_syn peak、峰曲率、L_bol、Compton dominance Y 等参数之间的相关性及分类型差异。  
- 提供了基于 γ 射线光变时滞的多普勒因子下限估算，为直接与 γ 射线发射区相关的喷射物速度诊断提供新的证据。

4. 方法概览  
- 数据：选取 93 例 Fermi blazars（56 FSRQ，35 BL Lacs，2 例不确定类型），构建准同时宽带 SED。  
- 拟合：对两峰使用 cubic（立方）函数拟合，以允许峰形潜在的非对称性。  
- 相关性分析：考察 ν_syn peak、峰曲率、L_bol、Compton dominance Y 等参数之间的关系，区分 FSRQ 与 BL Lacs 的模式差异。  
- 多普勒因子估计：利用 0.1–1 GeV 与 1–300 GeV 光变曲线的时滞，给出 4 个源的下限多普勒因子。

5. 任务与性能  
- 任务：通过准同时多波段数据构建并拟合 SED，深入探讨参数相关性与 blazar 序列的物理含义；并尝试以光变时滞为工具进行喷管速度诊断。  
- 性能/结论要点：  
  - ν_syn peak 与峰曲率关系弱，支持粒子加速的混合机制。  
  - 在观测者框架中，L_bol 与 Y 对 ν_syn peak 呈负相关；经过去放大后，L_bol 与 ν_syn peak 呈弱正相关；FSRQ 与 BL Lacs 显示不同相关模式，暗示冷却机制差异。  
  - 通过 γ 射线光变时滞给出 4 个源的下限多普勒因子，提供基于 γ 射线发射区的喷射速度诊断基准。

## Characterizing the origins of gamma-ray variability of the jetted active galactic nuclei observed with the Fermi-LAT
- ArXiv: 2605.20843v1
- Link: http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20843v1
- Updated (UTC): 2026-05-20T07:32:10+00:00
- Authors: Yongyun Chen, Qiusheng Gu, Junhui Fan, Dingrong Xiong, Xiaoling Yu, Xiaogu Zhong, Xiaotong Guo, Nan Ding
- Abstract: We conducted an analysis of gamma-ray variability in a large sample of jetted active galactic nuclei (AGNs) by utilizing archival Fermi-LAT light curves and applying damped random walk modeling to obtain variability amplitude. Our primary findings are summarized as follows: (1) The mean variability damping timescales of our sources are approximately 100 days. This damping timescale may imply that the diffusive shock acceleration plays an important role in the variability of gamma-ray emission. (2) Flat-spectrum radio quasars (FSRQs) demonstrate greater variability amplitude compared to BL Lacertae objects (BL Lacs). (3) The ratio of the distance of the emission region from the central supermassive black hole to the dusty torus radius for our sources is $R\approx2-4.5R_{\rm DT}$. In contrast, the ratio of the distance of the emission region from the central supermassive black hole to the BLR radius for our sources is $R\approx135-295R_{\rm BLR-in}$ and $R\approx123-270R_{\rm BLR-out}$. These findings indicate that the $γ$-ray emission region in jetted AGNs is likely located beyond the BLR, potentially could be associated with the dusty torus. (4) A statistical correlation is observed between variability amplitude and radio luminosity, radio loudness, X-ray luminosity, X-ray loudness, gamma-ray luminosity, and gamma-ray loudness, indicating a potential relationship between gamma-ray variability and jet activity. (5) Variability amplitude also shows a statistical correlations with synchrotron peak frequency luminosity, inverse Compton peak frequency luminosity, and Compton dominance. (6) Variability amplitude also correlates with black hole mass, accretion disk luminosity, and Eddington ratio, implying that the accretion disk may also contribute to gamma-ray variability.

**Translated Abstract**
我们利用档案中的 Fermi-LAT 光曲线，对大量 jetted AGN 的 gamma 射线变异性进行了分析，并应用 damped random walk（DRW）建模来获得变异幅度。我们的主要发现如下：(1) 观测样本的平均变异阻尼时间尺度约为100天。这一阻尼时间尺度可能意味着扩散性冲击加速在 gamma 射线发射的变异中起到重要作用。 (2) Flat-spectrum radio quasars (FSRQs) 的变异幅度高于 BL Lacertae 对象（BL Lacs）。 (3) 发射区距离中央超大质量黑洞的距离与尘埃圆环半径的比值为 R ≈ 2–4.5 R_DT；相比之下，发射区距离与 BLR 半径的比值为 R ≈ 135–295 R_BLR-in 和 R ≈ 123–270 R_BLR-out。这些发现表明 jetted AGN 的 γ 射线发射区域很可能位于 BLR 之外，可能与尘埃圆环相关。 (4) 变异幅度与射线亮度、射线响度、X 射线亮度、X 射线响度、γ 射线亮度、γ 射线响度之间存在统计相关性，表明 γ 射线变异性与喷流活动之间可能存在联系。 (5) 变异幅度还与 synchrotron peak frequency 亮度、inverse Compton peak frequency 亮度以及 Compton dominance 存在统计相关性。 (6) 变异幅度也与黑 hole 质量、吸积盘亮度和 Eddington 比值相关，暗示吸积盘也可能对 γ 射线变异产生贡献。

**Summary**
1. Research background
- Jetted AGN 的 gamma 射线变异性是理解喷流物理、发射区域位置及加速机制的重要线索。通过系统分析大样本的 γ 射线光曲线，可以揭示变异特征与多波段物理量之间的联系，从而推断发射区与驱动过程。

2. Prior methods and limitations
- 以往对 gamma 射线变异性的研究多依赖于传统的统计量或时序分析（如结构函数、功率谱密度等），在量化变异幅度与物理参数之间的直接关系、以及在不同射线源类型间的差异方面存在局限。缺乏一种能够将变异性参数直接映射到发射区域位置与喷流动力学的统一建模框架。

3. Main contributions
- 通过对大量 jetted AGN 的 Fermi-LAT 光曲线进行分析，结合 damped random walk（DRW）建模，系统提取变异幅度并对源类型、发射区位置以及多波段物理量之间的相关性进行了综合评估。研究给出关于发射区位置、变异驱动机制以及与喷流活动和吸积过程之间关系的定量证据。

4. Method overview
- 数据与样本：使用档案中的 Fermi-LAT 光曲线，覆盖大样本的 jetted AGN。
- 变异性定量：应用 DRW 模型来估计变异幅度及阻尼时间尺度。
- 发射区定位：将发射区相对于 BLR、 dusty torus 的距离比值（R ≈ 距离/ BLR 半径、R_DT）进行推断，评估发射区域在 BLR 外的可能性，以及与 dusty torus 的关联性。
