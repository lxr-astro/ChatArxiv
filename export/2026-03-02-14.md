## Direct VLBI Detection of Interstellar Turbulence Imprint on a Quasar: TXS 2005+403
- ArXiv: 2602.24255v1
- Link: http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.24255v1
- Updated (UTC): 2026-02-27T18:29:38+00:00
- Authors: Alexander Plavin, Alexander Pushkarev, Yuri Kovalev
- Abstract: We report the first unambiguous detection of refractive substructure in an active galactic nucleus (AGN) using ground-based Very Long Baseline Interferometry (VLBI). Our analysis of TXS 2005+403 - observed at 1--5 GHz along a line of sight through the Cygnus region - reveals clear signatures of turbulence-induced substructure on long baselines that cannot be explained by the smooth scatter-broadened profile from diffractive effects alone. This signal persists across multiple observations spanning 2010-2019, demonstrating stable scattering properties along the line of sight. The combination of high flux density, compact intrinsic structure, and strong scattering establishes TXS 2005+403 as an exceptional laboratory for probing Galactic turbulence. This detection demonstrates that AGNs can serve as cosmic lighthouses illuminating interstellar plasma across the sky, complementing pulsar scintillation studies and informing scattering mitigation for millimeter-wavelength imaging of Sagittarius A*.

**Translated Abstract**  
我们首次在一个活跃星系核 (AGN) 上，使用地基 VLBI 取得对折射子结构的明确检测。我们对 TXS 2005+403 的分析——在 1–5 GHz 的观测中，沿着穿过 Cygnus 区的视线——揭示了在长基线上的由湍流引起的子结构的清晰信号，这些信号不能仅由由衍射效应导致的平滑散射轮廓来解释。该信号在跨越 2010–2019 的多次观测中持续存在，表明视线上的散射特性是稳定的。高通量、紧凑的内在结构以及强散射共同使 TXS 2005+403 成为探测银河湍流的卓越实验室。这一发现表明 AGN 可以作为宇宙灯塔，照亮遍布天空的星际等离子体，补充了脉冲星散射研究，并为 Sagittarius A* 的毫米波成像的散射抑制提供信息。

---

**Summary**  
1. 研究背景  
- 星际介质中的湍流水平会引起电波的散射与闪烁效应，影响射电观测的成像和精度。  
- 通过长基线 VLBI，研究者可以探测到由湍流引起的亚结构，从而实现对银河平面的散射介质的成像和表征。  
- 以往在 AGN 或 pulsar 的观测中，关于折射性亚结构的明确检测仍然有限，且需要在高信噪比、紧凑源结构和强散射条件下才能实现。  
- Cygnus 区域的视线提供了测试 Galactic turbulence 的重要天区之一，而 TXS 2005+403 具备较高的通量密度和紧凑结构，使其成为理想的观测对象。

2. 现有方法与局限性  
- 以往多依赖脉冲星散射研究来探测星际湍流，而对 AGN 的直接探测相对稀少。  
- 许多观测只能观测到衍射性散射导致的平滑轮廓，难以区分或解析折射性亚结构。  
- 受限于数据质量、观测时间覆盖和源的固有变动性，难以在多次观测中确证稳定的折射信号。

3. 主要贡献  
- 提出并给出对 AGN 上折射性亚结构的第一份明确、无歧义的检测证据，首次在地基 VLBI 数据中直接观测到。  
- 以 TXS 2005+403 为对象，证实在 1–5 GHz、沿 Cygnus 区视线的长基线上存在由湍流引起的亚结构信号，且不能用仅由 diffractive 效应造成的平滑散射轮廓来解释。  
- 观测结果在跨越 2010–2019 的多次观测中保持稳定，表明散射性质在该视线长时间尺度上具有可重复性。  
- 强调 AGN 作为“宇宙灯塔”的潜力，拓展了对 Galactic 湍流的探测手段，辅以 pulsar 效应研究，并对 Sagittarius A* 的毫米波成像的散射抑制提供了新的信息。

4. 方法概览  
- 数据与对象：TXS 2005+403，在 1–5 GHz 的观测频段，沿穿过 Cygnus 区的视线进行 VLBI 观测；重点关注长基线上的信号结构。  
- 观测与比较：比较观测得到的子结构信号与仅由衍射性散射产生的平滑轮廓，寻找无法被后者解释的折射性亚结构证据。  
- 时间覆盖：综合分析2010、2011、2019等多次观测，评估信号的时域稳定性与散射性质的一致性。  
- 结论判断：若观测信号在长基线、在多 epochs 下持续且独立于源的内在结构变动，则可认为是湍流引起的折射性亚结构的证据。

5. 任务与表现  
- 任务：在地基 VLBI 条件下，通过对 TXS 2005+403 的多次观测，检出并确认折射性亚结构，进而实现对星际湍流的直接探针。  
- 表现与意义：首次在 AGN 上实现对折射性亚结构的无歧义探测，并证实源具备高通量、紧凑结构与强散射的有利条件，从而将 AGN 作为银河湍流探测的有力工具。这一成果为星际等离子体成像的散射校正与银河湍流成像提供新途径，且可与脉冲星散射研究互补，丰富对 Galactic turbulence 的全局理解。

## Weak emission line quasar SDSS J101353.45+492758.1 I. Continuum fitting
- ArXiv: 2602.24217v1
- Link: http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.24217v1
- Updated (UTC): 2026-02-27T17:44:42+00:00
- Authors: Laetitia Gibaud, Marek Nikołajuk, Piotr Życki, Agata Różańska, Krzysztof Hryniewicz, Rafał Wojaczyński
- Abstract: We present a broadband study of the WLQ SDSS J101353.45+492758.1, which displays a nearly featureless UV-optical spectrum with only a weak Mg II line alongside an exceptionally low X-ray flux. We model its spectral energy distribution using the relativistic thin-disk model kerrbb with a power law, and the multicomponent AGN model relagn, a physically motivated extension of agnsed incorporating warm and hot Comptonizing regions. Our fits constrain the black hole mass, accretion rate, X-ray loudness, and coronal energetics. Both approaches yield consistent BH masses of M_{BH} \approx 2 \times 10^{9} M_\odot and an Eddington accretion rate of \dot m \approx 0.1. The relagn fit including a warm Comptonizing region provides a significantly improved representation of the UV-soft X-ray continuum. The warm corona, characterized by kTe \simeq 0.20 keV, Γ \simeq 3.8, and an optical depth τ \simeq 7.26, extends to \sim 34 R_g. The hot corona appears compact and energetically suppressed, leading to an intrinsically weak X ray output with log(L_{X}/L_{bol}) \simeq -4.29, among the lowest reported for WLQs. The α_{ox} \sim 2.06 indicates the source to be in high/soft AGN spectral state. The combination of a luminous, standard disk and extremely weak hot corona suggests that this quasar hosts a highly inefficient inner coronal region. This explains its X-ray faintness and extreme deficit of high-ionization emission lines. The source may represent an AGN analog in "ultrasoft" accretion state, or a system in which the ionizing continuum is suppressed by a compact or quenched corona. Our study suggests that the source is not accreting at high Eddington ratio, highlighting the physical diversity of WLQs, and supports the view that geometric and radiative effects jointly shape their extreme spectral properties.

**Translated Abstract**
我们对弱发射线类星体 WLQ SDSS J101353.45+492758.1 进行了广带研究，该对象的紫外-可见光谱几乎无显著特征，只有一个微弱的 Mg II 线，并且 X 射线通量异常低。我们用相对论薄盘模型 Kerrbb 叠加幂律分布来建模其光谱能量分布，同时使用多分量 AGN 模型 relagn（对 agnsed 的物理扩展，包含暖 Compton 区域与热 Compton 区域）来拟合。拟合结果约束了黑洞质量、吸积速率、X 射线的强度以及珈层的能量。两种方法给出的黑洞质量一致约 M_BH ≈ 2 × 10^9 M⊙，Eddington 归一化吸积率约 dot m ≈ 0.1。包含暖 Compton 区的 relagn 拟合对紫外-软 X 射线连续光谱有显著改进。暖色层特征为 kTe ≈ 0.20 keV、Γ ≈ 3.8、τ ≈ 7.26，延伸至约 34 R_g。热色层显得紧凑且能量受抑制，导致 X 射线输出本来就很弱，log(L_X/L_bol) ≈ -4.29，属于 WLQ 中较低的水平之一。α_ox ≈ 2.06 指出该源处于高/软态 AGN 谱状态。标准盘的高光度与极弱热 corona 的组合提示该宽区黑洞在内层 corona 区域能效极低。这解释了其 X 射线微弱以及高离子化线的极端缺失。该源或 represent 在“超软”吸积态的 AGN 类比，或处于一个因紧凑或抑制的 corona 而使电离辐射连续体被削弱的系统。我们的研究表明此源并非在高 Eddington 比例下吸积，强调 WLQ 的物理多样性，并支持几何与辐射效应共同塑形其极端光谱特征的观点。

**Summary**
1. Research background
- WLQ（Weak emission line quasar）是一类发射线极弱的准恒星黑洞系统，具有近似无特征的紫外-可见光谱和异常低的 X 射线输出。这类对象对理解吸积盘、珈层结构以及辐射过程在极端条件下的表现具有重要意义，但由于谱线极弱，直接通过常规方法（如 Mg II、 Hβ 等谱线）测量黑洞质量与吸积状态比较困难，因此需要依赖广带光谱拟合来解析其中心引力源的物理性质。  
- 本研究以 WLQ SDSS J101353.45+492758.1 为对象，结合紫外-可见到 X 射线的广带观测，尝试通过不同的物理模型来同时约束黑洞质量、吸积速率与珈层结构。

2. Prior methods and limitations
- 以往在 WLQ 的研究中，往往依赖标准薄盘模型或简单的 X 射线能谱模型来解释整体光谱，难以同时解释紫外-软 X 射线连续体以及发射线极弱的原因。  
- 由于缺乏强谱线信息，单一的估算方法往往对黑洞质量和吸积率存在较大不确定性，且难以揭示暖/热 Compton 区域（暖 corona 与热 corona）的物理角色及其对能量预算的影响。  
- 现有方法在解释极低的 L_X/L_bol、α_ox、以及 WLQ 特有的极端离子化线缺失方面往往力不从心，需采用更物理化、可解释的多区域 Comptonization 框架来改进。

3. Main contributions
- 给出两种独立但相互一致的黑洞质量估计：M_BH ≈ 2 × 10^9 M⊙，以及 Eddington 归一化吸积率 dot m ≈ 0.1，显示该 WLQ 虽光谱极端，但并非处于超高 Eddington 状态。  
- 通过 relagn 模型（包含暖 Compton 区）对紫外-软 X 射线连续体给出显著更好的拟合，相对于仅暖/热 corona 的简化模型，能更真实地再现能谱形状。  
- 给出暖 corona 的物理参数：kTe ≈ 0.20 keV、Γ ≈ 3.8、τ ≈ 7.26，覆盖范围扩展到 ~34 R_g，强调其在能量传输与光谱形状中的关键作用。  
- 发现热 corona 为紧凑且能量输出受抑，导致 intrinsic X 射线极弱，L_X/L_bol 的对数值约为 -4.29，属于 WLQ 中极端低值之一，并给出 α_ox ≈ 2.06，指示源处于高/软态。  
- 结合结果提出该源可能处于“ ultrasoft”吸积态的 AGN 类比，或是在紧凑/抑制的 corona 架构下使离子化辐射连续体被削弱的情形，显示 WLQ 的物理多样性及几何与辐射效应共同决定其极端谱特征。

4. Method overview
- 采用两条并行的建模路径：一条使用相对论薄盘模型 Kerrbb + 一个幂律成分来描述整体光谱，另一条使用 relagn 这类对 agnsed 的物理扩展，内含暖 Compton 区和热 Compton 区的多组分 AGN 能谱模型。  
- 通过广带观测（包括 UV–optical 与 X 射线区），对黑洞质量、吸积速率、珈层能量预算、暖/热 corona 的参数进行联合拟合与自一致性检验。  
- 评估两种模型对紫外-软 X 射线连续体的拟合优度，尤其强调暖 corona 对光谱形状的作用，以及热 corona 的能量抑制对 X 射线输出的解释力。

5. Task and performance
- 任务：对 WLQ SDSS J101353.45+492758.1 的全波段（紫外到 X 射线）连续光谱进行物理 continuum fitting，以揭示隐藏在极端谱特征背后的黑洞参数与珈层结构。  
- performance（结果与意义）：两种建模给出一致的黑洞质量与吸积率，并且 relagn 模型在 UV–软 X 射线区提供更优的拟合，揭示暖 corona 的关键作用及热 corona 的抑制态。该 source 被解释为处于高/软态而非高 Eddington 比例，强调 WLQ 的物理多样性以及几何与辐射效应共同塑形其极端光谱特征的可能性。此工作为理解 WLQ 的 continuum shape 与内部物理机制提供了有力证据，并为未来对 WLQ 的系统性比较研究提供了重要基准。

## Filling the Gap in Cluster Evolution: JWST's Glimpse into a Young, Star-Forming Cluster at Cosmic Noon
- ArXiv: 2602.24162v1
- Link: http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.24162v1
- Updated (UTC): 2026-02-27T16:41:59+00:00
- Authors: Pierluigi Rinaldi, Stacey Alberts, Christopher N. A. Willmer, Courtney Carreira, Christina C. Williams, Gaël Noirot, Carys J. E. Gilbert, Andrew J. Bunker, William M. Baker, Luigi Barchiesi, Zhiyuan Ji, Jianwei Lyu, Sandro Tacchella, Zihao Wu, Yongda Zhu
- Abstract: We present a detailed study of HUDFJ0332.4-2746.6 (HUDF46), a $z \approx 1.84$ overdensity in the Hubble Ultra Deep Field, previously identified with HST as a proto-cluster. JWST/NIRISS spectroscopy expands its confirmed membership from 18 to 37 galaxies, while deep HST/ACS, JWST/NIRCam, and JWST/MIRI imaging provide a comprehensive multiwavelength view from the rest-frame UV to the mid-infrared. This dataset probes the population across three dex in stellar mass ($M_\bigstar \approx 10^{7.5\text{--}10.5}\,M_\odot$), delivering the first direct view of a young cluster down to such low-$M_\bigstar$ at $z\gtrsim1$. Assuming virialization, we derive a velocity dispersion of $σ\approx 670\pm 91\,\mathrm{km\,s^{-1}}$ and a halo mass of $M_{200} \approx (1.2\pm0.2) \times 10^{14}\,M_\odot$, in agreement with X-ray constraints from deep {\it Chandra} data. Despite residing in a massive halo likely in the hot-halo regime, the population is overwhelmingly star-forming, with no established red sequence and no extended X-ray emission from a hot intracluster medium. HUDF46 members have stellar and structural properties nearly indistinguishable from coeval field galaxies, and the structure hosts only one AGN candidate, found in its brightest galaxy, which lies at the cluster center. Overall, HUDF46 appears to be in a transitional phase prior to the onset of environmental quenching, making its galaxy population a key benchmark for tracing the processes that will later build a passive population and shape the assembly of massive clusters at later cosmic times.

## Multi-messenger emission derived from relativistic magnetized jet dynamics using a multi-zone framework
- ArXiv: 2602.24088v1
- Link: http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.24088v1
- Updated (UTC): 2026-02-27T15:25:17+00:00
- Authors: Harshita Bhuyan, Bhargav Vaidya, Christian Fendt, Aditya Sharma
- Abstract: Relativistic jets from Active Galactic Nuclei (AGN) are highly energetic and emit radiation across a wide range of frequencies. Despite several observational studies, their particle composition still remains a key open question. The detection of high-energy neutrinos from blazar sources such as TXS 0506+056 has highlighted the plausibility of hadronic/lepto-hadronic models for AGN jets. To understand the origin of high-energy neutrinos from such sources, it is imperative to capture the complex interplay between the jet dynamics, their composition, and the mechanism of particle acceleration and cooling in relativistic jets. In this pilot study, we have coupled a numerical multi-zone framework for lepto-hadronic modeling, with 3D relativistic magneto-hydrodynamic simulations of AGN jets, including external photon fields. Our framework provides synthetic multi-wavelength and neutrino flux by spatially sampling the simulated jet into multiple zones. We investigate the implications of such a framework in exploring the different intrinsic and extrinsic pathways for proton-enrichment in jets. Essentially, we find that for low proton-to-electron number density ratios, producing a substantial jet neutrino flux, requires the underlying proton energy distribution to have a relatively flat spectrum with a power-law index of $\simeq 2.0$. We further find that while intrinsic shocks triggered by kink-instabilities in the jet can accelerate electrons to high energies, they may not be sufficient to produce such flat particle energy distributions for the chosen set of parsec-scale jet parameters.
